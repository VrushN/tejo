Welcome to Our Sisterhood

A vibrant women's collective in Japan rooted in self-growth, community care, and empowerment. Proudly part of a larger legacy of change. Tejomayi is a sister organisation of Bharat Cultural Society (BCS) Japan. "Tejomayi" (तेजोमयी) is a Sanskrit word, means "she who is radiant energy" or "full of light," indicating a person that shines with divine brilliance.

Our Mission

To empower women by fostering personal growth, nurturing children's development, promoting effective parenting, and preserving the rich heritage of Indian culture and history. We strive to create a supportive community where women and families can thrive, ensuring a harmonious blend of tradition and progress.

Our Vision

To be a leading organization that transforms the lives of women and families in Japan, inspiring them to realize their full potential. We envision a society where empowered women lead the way in nurturing future generations, upholding the values of Indian culture and history, and contributing to a more equitable and enlightened world.
